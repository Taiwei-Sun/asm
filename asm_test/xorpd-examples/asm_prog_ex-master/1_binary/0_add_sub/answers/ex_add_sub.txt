  0.5   Bonus: How much is {8}_10 * {1101}_2 ? (in base 2)
        Note: * means multiplication.

    ANSWER:

    1101 * {2}_10 = 11010
    1101 * {2}_10 * {2}_10 = 110100
    1101 * {2}_10 * {2}_10 * {2}_10 = 1101 * {8}_10 = 1101000
